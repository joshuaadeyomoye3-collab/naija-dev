About the Conference

Why NaijaDevs?

Since 2019, NaijaDevs Conference has been the heartbeat of Nigeria's tech ecosystem. What started as a small meetup of 80 developers in Yaba has grown into a nationally recognised annual event drawing talent from across West Africa and the global diaspora. We believe that African developers are not just consumers of technology — we are builders of the future.

The 2026 edition carries the theme: "From Local to Global" — celebrating Nigerian engineers and founders who are shipping products used worldwide, while welcoming international speakers who want to learn from Africa's unique tech scene.

Whether you are a fresh graduate writing your first API, a seasoned architect designing distributed systems, or a founder raising your Series A — there is a track, a workshop, and a community for you at NaijaDevs 2026.
Come hungry. Leave inspired.
