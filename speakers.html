Speaker Bios

Adaeze Okonkwo

Staff Engineer and AI Lead, Kuda Bank

Adaeze leads a team at Kuda Bank building ML models for credit scoring and fraud detection. She holds a Master's in Computer Science from the University of Lagos and contributes regularly to TensorFlow's documentation.

Talk Topics

  • Building AI on Nigerian data
  • Practical ML pipelines with limited infrastructure

Back to speaker list

Emeka Nwosu

Principal Backend Engineer, Flutterwave

Emeka has been architecting payment infrastructure at Flutterwave for 5 years, leading the team behind the core payments API serving 34 African countries. He also runs a developer newsletter with 12,000 subscribers.

Talk Topics

  • Designing APIs that last
  • Database optimisation for high-throughput fintech

Back to speaker list

Fatima Bello

Senior DevOps Engineer, Microsoft Africa

Fatima is based in Abuja and specialises in IaC using Terraform and Azure DevOps. She co-organises the DevOps Nigeria community and has helped three startups migrate to cloud-native architectures.

Talk Topics

  • Zero to cloud: a startup's migration playbook
  • CI/CD pipelines on a budget

Back to speaker list

Olumide Adeyemi

Founder and CTO, BuildFast Lagos

Olumide founded BuildFast Lagos, a product studio that has shipped 14 live products in three years. He benchmarks every web app he ships above 90 on all Lighthouse metrics, even on 3G connections.

Talk Topics

  • Frontend performance for low-bandwidth Nigeria
  • Building design systems with CSS custom properties

Back to speaker list

Chisom Ezenwachi

Mobile Lead (Flutter), PiggyVest

Chisom leads mobile development at PiggyVest, one of Nigeria's most used fintech apps with over 5 million users. She led the migration to Flutter in 2023, reducing mobile team size by 40% while improving release speed.

Talk Topics

  • Flutter in production: what the tutorials don't tell you
  • Offline-first design for patchy networks

Back to speaker list

Tunde Kehinde

Open Source Advocate and GDE, Lagos

Tunde is a GDE for Web Technologies and maintains 6 widely used npm packages. He has contributed to V8, Deno, and the WHATWG HTML spec.

Talk Topics

  • Contributing to open source from Africa
  • Growing a developer community from 20 to 2,000
